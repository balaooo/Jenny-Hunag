My Story

Art History Student

I’ve been a huge fan of museum, since I was a child. After growing up, I found that museums are places where knowledge can be enjoyed by people of all classes. That’s why I choose to pursue my master degree in Art History.

Switch to Product

While working as an intern at NPM and participating in the online curator contest, I found online platforms more efficient to share knowledge. However, the usability still needed improvement, so I delved into its UX.

Designer in Finance

Having the goal to design products, I also equipped myself with web development skills. I had an open-choice when searching job, and now I work in a bank as a web designer.
